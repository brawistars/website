How much does it cost to renovate a one-room apartment?

How much it costs to renovate a one-room apartment depends on a set of factors: the list and complexity of repair and finishing work, area of ​​housing, selected materials, timing, etc. The cheapest renovation of a one-room apartment, as a rule, is done in housing for rent, and the most expensive is in the case of creating an exclusive designer interior with redevelopment using premium materials. If the apartment has a non-standard configuration, for example, with an attic, this is also reflected in the increase in the price tag of the renovation.

Photo of apartment renovation with attic

In general, the cost of repairing a one-room apartment of standard sizes is in the range of 30-45 square meters. m ranges from 100 to 300 thousand rubles, taking into account materials. With a non-standard configuration, the price can reach up to 400 thousand rubles. Terms vary from 30 to 45 days depending on the type of work and its volume.

How much does it cost to renovate a one-room apartment for rent?

The most economical renovation option is done in apartments for rent. Here, universal, standard solutions are used, as this affects the liquidity of the property in the rental market. The more standardized the housing, the higher the demand for it. There is no need to use expensive materials or remodel for this.

An example of a simple renovation for delivery

Simple, practical and wear-resistant materials are used for finishing. It must be possible to quickly, cheaply and efficiently restore damaged surfaces. In this regard, it is better to use painting instead of wallpaper, and linoleum should be laid on the floor throughout the entire apartment, with the exception of the bathroom, rather than laminate and tiles.
